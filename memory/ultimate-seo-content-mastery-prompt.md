## PHASE 5: ULTRA-HUMAN WRITING MASTERY

### The 10-Sentence Pattern (MEMORIZE THIS)
Every 10 sentences:
- 4 SHORT (3-7 words): Impact, emphasis
- 4 MEDIUM (10-18 words): Context, clarity
- 2 LONG (25-45 words): Complex entity relationships

### Contractions (95%+ MANDATORY)
Always: don't, can't, won't, it's, I've, I'm, you're, we've, they're, hasn't, doesn't, wouldn't, couldn't, shouldn't

### Casual Sentence Starters (50%)
Use constantly: Look, So, And, But, Well, Now, Okay, Yeah, See, Plus, Actually, Honestly, Basically

### Personal Voice (Every Paragraph)
Replace objective with personal:
- "I've found that..."
- "In my experience..."
- "I've seen..."
- "We've tested..."
- "I think..."
- "My take on this is..."

### Rhetorical Questions (5-8 per article)
Pattern: [Question]? [Short answer]. [Explanation].

### Sentence Fragments (8-12 per article)
- "Really powerful."
- "Not even close."
- "The best part? This."

### Parenthetical Asides (5-8 per article)
"(and honestly, it's kind of amazing)"
"(which surprised me)"
"(trust me on this one)"
"(I know, I know, sounds crazy)"

### Conversational Fillers
Kind of, sort of, like, basically, actually, really, just, honestly, literally, seriously, obviously, definitely, probably, maybe

### Natural Word Repetition
Humans repeat. AI synonyms.
‚úì GOOD: "This is good. Really good."
‚úó BAD: "This is excellent. Truly outstanding."

### Intentional Grammar Breaks
- Start sentences with: And, But, So, Or
- End with prepositions
- Use "who" not "whom"
- Split infinitives

### Varied Paragraph Length
- 20% one-sentence
- 40% two-sentence
- 30% three-to-four-sentence
- 10% five-plus-sentence
