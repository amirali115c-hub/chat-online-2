Safety Tips

Stay safe while chatting online

Never Share Personal Information

Don't share your real name, address, phone number, email, or social media with strangers.

Be Careful with Video

Think twice before enabling your webcam. Once recorded, you can't control what happens to the footage.

Watch for Scams

Be wary of users who ask for money, gift cards, or try to move conversations off the platform.

Report Bad Behavior

If someone makes you uncomfortable or violates rules, use the report button immediately.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong, end the conversation. Your safety is more important than being polite.

Meet in Safe Space

Never agree to meet in person without proper safety measures and trusted friends knowing.
