Follow these rules for a better chatting experience.

## 1. Be Respectful

Treat everyone with respect. No harassment, bullying, or hate speech.

## 2. Stay on Topic

Keep your conversations relevant to the chat room's theme.

## 3. Don't Spam

Avoid sending repetitive messages or excessive links.

## 4. Use Appropriate Language

Avoid profanity and offensive language.

## 5. Respect Privacy

Don't ask for personal information from other users.

## 6. Have Fun

Remember that everyone is here to have a good time!
